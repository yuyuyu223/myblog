∇v=∂vn∂x∣x=xpn≠0\left.\nabla\boldsymbol{v}=\frac{\partial\boldsymbol{v}^n}{\partial \boldsymbol{x}}\right|_{\boldsymbol{x}=\boldsymbol{x}_p^n} \neq0 ∇v=∂x∂vn​∣∣∣∣∣​x=xpn​​​=0
